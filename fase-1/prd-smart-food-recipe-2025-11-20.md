# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

**Date:** 2025-11-20
**Project:** Smart Food & Recipe Platform
**Command:** `/pm *prd`

## 1. Overview
Smart Food & Recipe Platform is a Next.js application for intelligent meal planning.

## 2. Goals
- Simplify meal planning
- Reduce food waste
- Improve nutrition

## 3. User Stories
- As a user, I want to search recipes based on ingredients
- As a user, I want to generate weekly meal plans
- As a user, I want to export shopping lists

## 4. Functional Requirements
| ID | Requirement | Priority |
|----|------|-----------|
| FR-001 | Recipe Search | High |
| FR-002 | Meal Planner | High |
| FR-003 | Shopping List | Medium |

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- Response time < 2s
- 99.9% uptime
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility
